The objective of the game is to get savings to equal GOAL. The options are, giveUp(), which means to give up, newRandomCurrentNumber(), which sets currentRandomNumber to a pseudorandomly-generated number, savings += currentRandomNumber, savings -= currentRandomNumber, savings *= currentRandomNumber, and savings /= currentRandomNumber, which mean to add currentRandomNumber to savings, to subtract currentRandomNumber to savings, to multiply currentRandomNumber by savings, and to divide currentRandomNumber by savings respectively, and the last is removeDecimalPoints(), which removes any decimal points from added when dividing.
